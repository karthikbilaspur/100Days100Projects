# 🤖 MediBot: Your Personal AI Health Assistant 🩺

---

Hey Health Explorers! 👋 Super excited to share my latest project with you: MediBot! 🚀

This isn't just any chatbot; it's your go-to AI companion for quick, reliable general health info, symptom guidance, and advice on common ailments. All built from scratch with just HTML, CSS, and Vanilla JavaScript! No frameworks, just pure coding power! 💪

---

## ✨ Features You'll Love:

   💬 Interactive Chat Interface: Clean, intuitive, and designed for easy conversations.
   🧠 Smart Responses: My brain (aka `knowledgeBase`) is structured with regex for more accurate and contextual answers.
   💡 Context-Aware: Remembers the last topic to give you more relevant follow-ups!
   ⏳ Typing Indicator: See when MediBot is "thinking" for a more natural chat flow.
   💾 Session Persistence: Your chat history is saved right in your browser (`localStorage`) – never lose a conversation!
   🚀 Quick Reply Buttons: Tap for instant answers to common questions.
   📱 Fully Responsive: Looks amazing on your desktop, tablet, or phone (thanks, media queries!)
   🧹 Clear Chat: Need a fresh start? One click and you're good to go!
   💖 SEO & Social Ready: Optimized with meta descriptions, Open Graph, and Twitter Cards so you can easily share your health insights!
