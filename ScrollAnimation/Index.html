How to play

Simon will give you a command starting with "Simon says:". Your task is to remember it. After a short delay, you'll be prompted to "Do the command!". You then have a limited time to click the "I did it!" button. Be quick and accurate!

Note: This is a mental exercise game. You actually perform the actions yourself; there's no physical input other than clicking "I did it!".
