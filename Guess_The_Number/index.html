How to Play Guess the Number

Goal: Guess the secret number the computer has picked!

  • Choose a difficulty level, which sets the number range and your allowed guesses.
  • Click "Start Game" to begin.
  • Enter your guess in the input field and click "Guess" or press Enter.
  • You'll get feedback: "Too high!", "Too low!", or "You got it!".
  • The background color will change based on how close your guess is.
  • On your last guess, you'll get an extra hint: whether the number is even or odd!
  • Try to get the number in the fewest guesses to earn a high score!

Good luck!
